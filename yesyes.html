Description

Shreeji interior doors are a premium product of a shreeji Interior. made from Shreeji interior Solid PVC Foam profiles with integral skin manufactured at a state of the art factory using breakthrough technology from ITALY to suit Indian condition.

Shreeji interior doors are sturdy, elegant, prefinished, durable and long lasting. Shreeji interior doors are provided with structurally strong stiffener frame and sandwich paneled to offer heat and sound insulation. High pressure laminate/imported fiber skin infill panel provides wide variety of elegant designs with natural wood like surface finish and scratch resistance. Etched or floral designed glass infill panels can also be provided. Arched type and louvered or diamond look type profiled door in fills can be offered.

Shreeji interior is as elegant as any premium wood in appearance it is equally workable. can be nailed, screwed, drilled, glued, sawn, tapped or welded just like wood. Shreeji interior comes in a range of different shapes, colors & finishes. Shreeji interior is characterized by excellent screw holding strength (>200 Kgf.) and shore hardness A of more than 70.
